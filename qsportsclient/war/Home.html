Why should I give my Google account information?
In order to give controlled access to information and privileges within Cricket Q user has to be registered with a user name and password. Since it is a overhead to remember an exclusive user name for Cricket Q, we decided to use your Google account information. Cricket Q chose Google for its security and privacy policy.
